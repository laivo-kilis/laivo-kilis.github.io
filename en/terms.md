The Client must provide accurate contact information when booking. Incorrect information may result in cancellation of the reservation.

## 3. Payment

The full amount must be paid before the service date or as agreed with the Operator.

A **300 EUR deposit** is collected at the start of the rental. The deposit is refunded upon return of the boat in good condition and with a full fuel tank.

## 4. Fuel Policy

The boat is rented with a full tank of fuel. It must be returned with a full tank. If the boat is returned without a full tank, fuel costs will be deducted from the deposit.

If the service cannot be provided due to the Operator's fault, the advance payment is refunded in full or an alternative service is offered.
